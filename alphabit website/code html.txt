        <h1>About<span>Club</span></h1>
        <p>
          Alphabit Club is a group of ambitious ESI-SBA students who generate,
          advance and share interests in the field of computer science and
          information science and technology. The members of the club meet
          regularly to decide on activities which consist of helping students in
          many areas, from providing all kinds of training and advice needed, to
          cultural and entertainment activities they need from time to time.
        </p>
